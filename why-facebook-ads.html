Why Facebook Ads?

by kim@kimlmedia.com

Posted on May 19th, 2020

Traditional ways of marketing are expensive and difficult to track. If you’ve downloaded this Ebook, you probably own a business yourself and know how much money it costs to pay for a radio or television ad. If you don’t, care to take a guess…?

Easily multiple thousands of dollars for 15-20 spots in a single week.

Using traditional marketing mediums like the radio, billboards, television and newspaper ads are like pouring money into a lake and hoping for the Loch Ness monster to fly out. You’re going to be standing there a v-e-e-e-ry long time.

Facebook ads are measurable, trackable, tangible and effective. You know how much money you spent, and how much money you made in return. On top of that, you know which ads performed better, at what time of the week, day, to which gender and age group, on which platform (Facebook owns Instagram, so both platforms host their ads in the same place) and dozens of other metrics.

It can cost as little as $5 to reach 1000 people scrolling through their phones, people who already have an affinity to your services. Do you think 1 in a 1000 people would be interested in what you have to share or offer?

Of course, they would!

Facebook is the perfect place for your business to build authority online by creating a long term funnel (more on that later in the book).

Imagine this: You just started a wedding cake business in your hometown of Nashville, Tennessee. You posted flyers, told all your friends about it, you even tried google ads. The few reviews you got are out of this world but something is holding you back from reaching your full potential.

What if I told you, you could target newly engaged, 25-40 year old women that work in the legal industry in your area? (We all know lawyers have money to spend on a fancy wedding cake!)

There are a plethora of choices at your disposition. You can target people based on location, high-income zip codes, online purchase behavior, iPhone users, Android users… and a lot more.
