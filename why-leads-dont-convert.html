Why Leads Don’t Convert On Your Website

by kim@kimlmedia.com

Posted on May 19th, 2020

Business owners tell me the same thing all the time. They feel their website converts just fine, and that’s why they never put any effort into making a landing page. Sound about right?

Wrong.

Websites don’t convert very well when it comes to paid ads, plain and simple. Your prospects need to click around to figure out how to send you a message or know more about you. We can’t afford to have prospects drop off because they got distracted.

With a landing page, your prospects can only do two things.

  1. Do what we want them to do.
  2. Leave.

People stop on your ad because it’s eye-catching and relevant. They want and need the service you are providing, so they click…

When they get on the page you want to drive your traffic to, we need to make it as easy as possible for them to give us their contact information. They don’t want to use any brainpower to do this.

They ONLY want to see the offer that’s promised to them and they want an easy way to get it. Check out these examples below…

Imagine this: You just started a wedding cake business in your hometown of Nashville, Tennessee. You posted flyers, told all your friends about it, you even tried google ads. The few reviews you got are out of this world but something is holding you back from reaching your full potential.

What if I told you, you could target newly engaged, 25-40 year old women that work in the legal industry in your area? (We all know lawyers have money to spend on a fancy wedding cake!)

Now, which page do you think converts better? The page that…

  • Clearly defines the offer 
  • Clearly defines the call to action
  • Give you more information about that offer

Or...

The page that requires you to navigate to other pages, click around, find the Contact Us section and inquire about the offer here.

Do you think you’ll even remember what the offer is by the time you get there?

You won't.

ALWAYS use a landing page when advertising online. Your results will be astronomically better.

There are many tools you can use to build one out or you can pay the pros (uh-hum) to build a highly converting landing page for you.
